ITZY Yuna - Scrambled Egg Toast

There are many ways to prepare simple toast with straberry and peanut butter jam but Yunas iteration surely is going to suit your cravings

Recipe and serving suggestions follow.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Strawberry Jam
  • Peanut Butter
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Pop the bread in the toaster and toast it to your likings. Yuna likes her toast crispy!
  2. Spread strawberry jam on about half of the bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on the other half of the bread.
  4. Top it off with fluffy scrambled eggs and you're done!
