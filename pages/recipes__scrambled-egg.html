ITZY Yuna - Scrambled Egg Toast

Description

There are many ways to prepare simple toast with straberry and peanut butter jam but Yunas iteration surely is going to suit your cravings

    Ingredients
  • Bread slices
  • Strawberry Jam
  • Peanut Butter
  • Eggs
      • Directions
  • Pop the bread in the toaster and toast it to your likings. Yuna likes her toast crispy!
  • Spread strawberry jam on about half of the bread.
  • Spread peanut butter on the other half of the bread.
  • Top it off with fluffy scrambled eggs and you're done!
