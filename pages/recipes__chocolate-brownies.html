SKZ Felix - Chocolate Brownies

    Ingredients
  • 6 chocolate bars
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 packet of instant coffee
  • 100 grams of flour
  • 100 grams of butter
      • Directions
  • Melt and mix the butter and the chocolate bars
  • Mix the eggs, sugar, coffee and flour
  • Mix boths mixtures
  • Bake at 200 degrees for 20 minutes
  • Let it rest for 20 minutes
  • Enjoy with a good cup of coffee
